यात्रा

Shimla, India

Known for its colonial architecture, scenic hills, and the famous Mall Road, Shimla offers a perfect blend of history and nature.

Explore Jakhoo Temple, enjoy toy train rides, and visit the Viceregal Lodge.

Gulmarg, India

Gulmarg, nestled in the heart of Kashmir, is a paradise for skiing enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Experience Gondola rides, lush meadows, and skiing at the world-class slopes.

Gangtok, India

Gangtok, the capital of Sikkim, offers stunning views of the Kanchenjunga, Buddhist monasteries, and vibrant markets.

Discover MG Marg, visit Rumtek Monastery, and explore Tsomgo Lake.
